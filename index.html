About IDO Consulting

Founded in 2015, IDO Consulting emerged from a vision to democratize access to world-class executive leadership. Our founders recognized that many growing businesses needed C-suite expertise but couldn't justify the cost of full-time executives. This insight led to the development of our innovative fractional leadership model.

Our mission is to empower organizations with strategic leadership that drives sustainable growth. We believe in delivering practical solutions, measurable results, and lasting impact through our values of excellence, integrity, innovation, and partnership. Every engagement is tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients, ensuring maximum value and transformation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is fractional leadership?

Fractional leadership provides experienced executive expertise on a part-time basis, allowing organizations to access C-suite talent without the full-time commitment and cost. This model is ideal for growing businesses that need strategic guidance but aren't ready for full-time executive hires.

How long is a typical engagement?

Our engagements typically range from 6 to 18 months, depending on your organization's needs and objectives. We work with you to determine the optimal duration and scope to achieve your strategic goals.

What roles do you provide?

We offer fractional executives across key C-suite positions, including CEO, CFO, COO, CTO, and CMO. Each leader brings extensive experience in their respective domains and a proven track record of success.

How do you measure success?

We establish clear KPIs and milestones at the outset of each engagement, tracking progress through regular reviews and assessments. Our focus is on delivering measurable business impact and sustainable growth.

What industries do you serve?

We work across various sectors, including technology, healthcare, manufacturing, and professional services. Our executives bring both industry-specific expertise and cross-sector insights to each engagement.
